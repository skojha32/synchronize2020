BOTSTATZ

Statistics

EVENT DETAILS(1ST YEAR)

ROUND 1: CROSSWORD

Rules:
• Registered participants will be added to a whatsapp group.
• A crossword link will be sent on the group..
• Time duration: 15 minutes.

ROUND 2: SPOT THE STATS WORD OUT!

Description: A story comprising statistical terms and concepts would be narrated to the participants via zoom meeting.Participants must pick the words while the narration is happening and note it down.Participants will be selected for the final round based on the number of right words collected and the time taken for the same,
Rules:
• Individual event.
• Time limit:40 minutes.
• Google form will be provided for the entries.
• The round will be conducted virtually on zoom.

FINAL ROUND: PICTOGRAPH

Description: Participants will have to guess the statistical terms from the picture that are displayed on the screen.
Rules:
• Individual event.
• Time limit:1 hour.
• The round will be conducted virtually through zoom.
• The answer should be privately sent to the host in the chat box and scores will be awarded accordingly.

EVENT DETAILS(2ND YEAR)

ROUND 1: APTITUDE

Rules:
• Registered participants will be added to a whatsapp group.
• A Google form will be sent on the group..
• Time duration: 15 minutes.

ROUND 2

Description: TASK 1: STATE THE THEOREM: Participants will be given a set of clues. They have to rearrange to state the theorem.
TASK 2: STATISTICAL PARADOX: This task will be a combination of chance and knowledge, Contain your curiousity until the event!(PS:Participants can research about paradoxes)
Rules:
• Individual event.
• Time limit:10+40 minutes.
• Google form will be provided for the entries(task 1).
• The round will be conducted virtually on zoom.

FINAL ROUND: STATISTICAL ANALYSIS

Description: A spreadsheet consisting of data will be shared with the participants. Winners will be decided based on what extent they analyse the data and the accuracy of the analysis.
Rules:
• Individual event.
• Time limit: 40 Mins
• The round will be conducted virtually through zoom.
• The answer should be saved as a file(.doc or .docx) and the google drive link should be submitted through the google form.
